UNCONDITIONAL CEASEFIRE

Since October 2023, the Palestinains are living in Hell. They are constanly forced to displace from one region to another. Lack of basic facilities and restricted access to humanitarian aid is making the situation more disasterous.

Air Strikes

Due to unaccounted Air strikes, chilren are being burried alive in the rubble. Those who survive will be crippled for the rest of their lives. The intentional targetting of relief camps (displaced with high population density) is a direct violation of international human rights

Last Air Strike was on 20/02/24

Bombing of Hospitals

The offensive has directly engaged in targetting humanitarian establishments primarily Hospitals. All major hospitals in the affected region have been reduced to rubble. Many chilren have been unearthed and found to be burried alive who were admitted in these hospitals.

Intentional Starvation

The issue of intentional starvation as a methof of warfare against inncoent chilren is a grave concern. This sitation has leads to malnutrition and associated diseases. The stress and taruma living in state of food insecurity has profound psychological impact on chilren.

Restrictions on Healthcare

The resitrictions on the evacuation of victims in air strikes and other offensives is increasing the innocent casualities in Palestine. Most of the humanitarian aid material is being denied entry into Palestine which has created panic and deteriorated the sitation further.

Use of White Phosphorous

The use of phosphorous bombs is considered illegal under international laws. It leads to vision impairment, respiratory distress and burns through the bodies of victims. The sustance continues to pierece the skin and will reach the bone unless properly washed out.
